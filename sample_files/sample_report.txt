Q1 2024 Financial Report
========================

Executive Summary
-----------------
This report covers the financial performance of DocFlow Inc. for the first quarter of 2024.
Revenue grew by 23% year-over-year, driven by strong enterprise adoption and new product launches.

Key Metrics
-----------
- Total Revenue: $4.2M (up 23% YoY)
- Operating Expenses: $2.8M
- Net Income: $1.4M
- Customer Count: 1,240 (up 18% YoY)
- Monthly Active Users: 8,500

Product Highlights
------------------
The document processing platform saw significant improvements in Q1:
1. Async processing pipeline reduced average processing time by 40%
2. New export formats (CSV, JSON) added based on customer feedback
3. Real-time progress tracking launched with WebSocket support

Outlook
-------
Q2 2024 looks promising with several enterprise deals in the pipeline.
We expect revenue to reach $5M by end of Q2.
